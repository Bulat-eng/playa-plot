Notes

North is up. The arrow marks BRC's prevailing wind/dust from the west–southwest — face pod and tent doors away from it and use RVs as a windbreak on the windward edge.

RV widths are with slides in. Each slide-out adds roughly 2–3 ft per side (up to ~5 ft on big fifth wheels) — resize an item to show yours extended.

50-ft increments is how Burning Man wants whole placed camps sized on the questionnaire; your hub's raw footprint feeds into your camp's total.

Fuel: keep 10′ clear of any ignition source, 20′ between liquid fuel and propane, 50′ from another fuel area.
